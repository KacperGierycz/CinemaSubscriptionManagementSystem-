<h3 class="western"><a name="__RefHeading___Toc870_3718250370"></a><font face="Arial, sans-serif">2.
Problem Statement</font></h3>
<p><font face="Arial, sans-serif">Customer seeks to introduce a
subscription service for partner cinemas within their existing
payment app. The service should allow users to subscribe to a cinema
and attend movies up to twice a week. This needs to be delivered
promptly to drive user engagement and growth while ensuring
scalability, security, and seamless integration.</font></p>
<p><font face="Arial, sans-serif">Key Assumptions:</font></p>
<ul>
	<li><p style="font-weight: normal; margin-bottom: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><i>Interview
	all Company stakeholders, for feedback and possible insight before
	contacting Customer.</i></font></p></li>
	<li><p style="font-weight: normal; margin-bottom: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><i>Brainstorm
	the team for estimated timeline and risks for this project.</i></font></p></li>
	<li><p style="font-weight: normal; margin-bottom: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif"><i>Prepare
	more detailed roadmap and setup the meeting with the Customer.</i></font></p></li>
	<li><p style="margin-bottom: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif">Integration
	with partner cinemas via their booking and payment systems.</font></p></li>
	<li><p style="margin-bottom: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif">Subscription
	management within Customer’s app.</font></p></li>
	<li><p style="margin-bottom: 0cm"><font face="Arial, sans-serif">Secure
	recurring billing for subscription plans (adhering to PCI DSS).</font></p></li>
	<li><p><font face="Arial, sans-serif">Cross-platform support
	(iOS/Android).</font></p></li>
</ul>

	<li><p style="border: none; padding: 0cm"><span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm">View

	</ul>
	<li><p style="border: none; padding: 0cm"><span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm">Secure
	Recurring Billing</span></p>
	<ul>
		<li><p style="border: none; padding: 0cm"><span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><strong>Given&nbsp;</strong><strong><span style="font-weight: normal">the&nbsp;user&nbsp;subscribes</span></strong></span></p></li>
		<li><p style="border: none; padding: 0cm"><span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><strong>Then</strong>&nbsp;the&nbsp;payment&nbsp;methods&nbsp;comply&nbsp;with&nbsp;PCI&nbsp;DSS&nbsp;requirements&nbsp;for&nbsp;secure&nbsp;recurring&nbsp;billing</span></p></li>
	</ul>
	<li><p style="border: none; padding: 0cm"><span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm">Handle

		<li><p style="border: none; padding: 0cm"><span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><strong>Then</strong>&nbsp;the&nbsp;user&nbsp;is&nbsp;notified&nbsp;and&nbsp;given&nbsp;retry&nbsp;options</span></p></li>
	</ul>

	</ul>

	<ul>
		<li><p style="border: none; padding: 0cm"><span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><strong>Given&nbsp;</strong><strong><span style="font-weight: normal">a&nbsp;failed&nbsp;card&nbsp;charge&nbsp;attempt</span></strong></span></p></li>
		<li><p style="border: none; padding: 0cm"><span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><strong>Then</strong>&nbsp;the&nbsp;system&nbsp;retries&nbsp;in&nbsp;24&nbsp;hours&nbsp;and&nbsp;notifies&nbsp;the&nbsp;user</span></p></li>
	</ul>
	<li><p style="border: none; padding: 0cm"><span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm">Subscription

	</ul>
</ol>
<p style="border: none; padding: 0cm"><span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><strong>Corner&nbsp;Cases:</strong></span></p>

	<li><p style="border: none; padding: 0cm"><span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><strong>Subscription&nbsp;Overlap</strong>:&nbsp;Handle&nbsp;scenarios&nbsp;where&nbsp;the&nbsp;user&nbsp;tries&nbsp;to&nbsp;subscribe&nbsp;again&nbsp;without&nbsp;knowing&nbsp;they're&nbsp;already&nbsp;subscribed,&nbsp;with&nbsp;clear&nbsp;messages.</span></p></li>

	<li><p style="border: none; padding: 0cm"><span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><strong>Partial&nbsp;Payments</strong>:&nbsp;Handle&nbsp;scenarios&nbsp;where&nbsp;users&nbsp;might&nbsp;partially&nbsp;complete&nbsp;payments&nbsp;due&nbsp;to&nbsp;connectivity&nbsp;issues&nbsp;or&nbsp;interruptions.</span></p></li>

	of payment method.</span></p>

		<li><p style="border: none; padding: 0cm"><span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><strong>Then</strong>&nbsp;the&nbsp;update&nbsp;process&nbsp;is&nbsp;secure&nbsp;and&nbsp;complies&nbsp;with&nbsp;PCI&nbsp;DSS&nbsp;standards</span></p></li>
	</ul>
	<li><p style="border: none; padding: 0cm"><span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm">Cancellation
	of a subscription.</span></p>

	</ul>

	payment failure.</span></p>

	</ul>

	refund in MVP</span></p>

		<li><p style="border: none; padding: 0cm"><span style="display: inline-block; border: none; padding: 0cm"><strong>Then</strong>&nbsp;refunds&nbsp;are&nbsp;not&nbsp;applicable,&nbsp;but&nbsp;future&nbsp;iterations&nbsp;will&nbsp;consider&nbsp;a&nbsp;more&nbsp;customer-friendly&nbsp;approach</span></p></li>

		multi-factor authentication (MFA) for users managing their

		audit the system to check for vulnerabilities and ensure

		failed payments gracefully with automated retry mechanisms and user
